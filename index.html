Closed-loop card dispensing robot (ECE 118). Self-fabricated chassis combining FDM printing and laser cutting. Hierarchical state machine coordinates servo scanning, player detection, and automated multi-player card dispensing.

PIC32 HSM / FSM Ultrasonic Laser Cutting L298 H-bridge
+
