Technical Details

Embedded Control

  • Architected a hierarchical state machine (HSM) in C using the ES Framework to coordinate servo scanning, player detection, and automated card dispensing.
  • Integrated ultrasonic sensing, servo positioning, and DC motor control (L298 H-bridge) into a cohesive embedded system on a custom soldered perfboard.
  • Interrupt-driven state transitions for reliable real-time cross-component synchronization.

Mechanical Design & Fabrication

  • Designed full chassis in Onshape using parametric CAD, combining FDM 3D printing and laser cutting.
  • Applied kerf compensation to laser-cut panels for precise press-fit assembly.
  • Iterated on servo mount geometry to ensure consistent card alignment and reliable ejection.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Multi-card dispensing failures — resolved through friction guide redesign and reverse motor logic to achieve consistent single-card output.
  • Wire management with multiple subsystems — addressed with structured perfboard layout and cable ties.
  • Ultrasonic detection false positives — tuned detection threshold and added debounce logic in firmware.

My Role

  • Led mechanical design and fabrication — chassis, servo mounts, and card ejection mechanism.
  • Contributed to HSM firmware architecture and sensor integration.
  • Diagnosed and resolved multi-card dispensing failures through combined mechanical and control fixes.
