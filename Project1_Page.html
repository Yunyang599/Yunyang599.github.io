Technical Details

Mechanical Design

  • Parametric CAD in Onshape — all joints designed for tolerance stack-up under 35 kg·cm load.
  • 25+ FDM-printed parts fabricated on Bambu Lab P1S; no laser cutting used.
  • 5+ build–test–iterate cycles to resolve inter-link alignment and servo clearance issues.
  • Base designed as a 5-leg star pattern for stability during high-torque operation.

Embedded Control

  • STM32-based servo control with PWM signal generation for all 7 joints.
  • Regulated buck power architecture to maintain stable voltage under full servo load.
  • Systematically characterized dead-band and pulse-width response per servo to reduce positioning error by ~30–50%.
  • Joystick teleoperation interface for real-time manual control of each joint.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Voltage sag under simultaneous multi-servo load — resolved with per-rail decoupling capacitors and regulated buck converter.
  • Joint backlash at high torque — addressed through tighter tolerance iteration in CAD and printed test fits.
  • Cable routing interference — redesigned link geometry to create internal cable channels.

My Role

  • Mechanical Lead — owned all CAD design and fabrication decisions.
  • Drove design review cycles and coordinated build schedule with team.
  • Contributed to servo calibration and embedded control testing.
